Overview

WHY DO I NEED THIS TRAINING?

To obtain your Ontario Security Guard License, you must be at least 18 years old, have a clean criminal record, complete a 40-hour training course (33.5 Security Guard Training and 6.5 First Aid Training), pass a 60-question multiple-choice exam, and submit your application to the Ministry.


This course consists of 33.5 hours of online study. A blended Emergency First Aid & CPR/AED training program must be taken in addition to this course to obtain your Security Guard License. A list of WSIB Ontario approved vendors can be found here.


WHY CHOOSE THIS COURSE?

This Security Guard Course provides all the essential knowledge required to pass your licensing exam, plus extra insights to boost your confidence on the job.


  • 12 in-depth modules covering everything you need to know.

By completing this program, you'll gain the skills and knowledge needed to protect people, respond to emergencies, and thrive in the security industry.

Each module is designed to give you the knowledge and skills required to excel in your security career! Upon completion of the Ministry-mandated requirements, you’ll be eligible to apply for your Ontario Security Guard License.
