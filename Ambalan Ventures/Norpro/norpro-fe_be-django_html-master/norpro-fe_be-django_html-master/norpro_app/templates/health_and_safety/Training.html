Lead Class 1 Operations Worker Training
The Lead Class 1 Operations Worker Training is a comprehensive full-day, in-person course designed for workers exposed to lead in construction and industrial settings. It covers critical topics such as lead properties, health effects, relevant legislation, and safe work practices, including personal protective equipment and hygiene standards. The training includes practical exercises to reinforce safety measures and ensure effective implementation in the workplace. At the end of the course, participants will complete a multiple-choice test, and those who pass will receive a certificate of completion. This training is vital for enhancing safety and compliance in lead operations. Register today to secure your spot! More Details
Lead Hazard Awareness Training
The Lead Hazard Awareness Training is a half-day, in-person course that educates workers and employers about the dangers of lead exposure in construction and industrial environments. Participants will learn to identify lead-containing materials and understand the associated health risks. The training covers lead properties, health effects, and relevant legislation, along with practical exercises to enhance understanding and retention. Attendees will receive a participant guide and worksheets, and upon completion, they will earn a certificate. This course is essential for anyone who may encounter lead-containing products, including supervisors and health and safety professionals. For scheduling and pricing, please contact us directly. Type 1 & 2 Asbestos Operations Worker Training
Type 1 & 2 Asbestos Operations Worker Training is a full-day, in-person course for workers who may encounter asbestos in construction and industrial settings. This training covers critical topics, including asbestos properties, health effects, relevant legislation, and safe work practices for Type 1 and Type 2 operations. Participants will learn to classify asbestos operations and implement control measures to minimize exposure effectively. The course includes practical exercises to reinforce safety protocols. At the end of the training, participants will complete a multiple-choice test, and those who pass will receive a certificate. This training is essential for anyone performing or supervising Type 1 and Type 2 operations. Register today to enhance workplace safety! More Details
Asbestos Hazard Awareness Training
Asbestos Hazard Awareness Training is a half-day, in-person course designed to educate workers and employers about the dangers of asbestos exposure in various environments. Participants will learn to identify asbestos-containing materials and understand health risks like asbestosis and lung cancer. The training covers asbestos properties, relevant legislation, and effective classification of operations. Practical exercises will enhance comprehension and retention of the material. Upon completion, participants will receive a certificate of achievement. This course is vital for anyone who may encounter asbestos, including supervisors and health and safety professionals.
