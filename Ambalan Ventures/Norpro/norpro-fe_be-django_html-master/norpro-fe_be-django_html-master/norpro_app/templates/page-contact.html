Frequently Asked Questions

As an Industrial Security Guard, your key duties/responsibilities include:

  • Providing security by protecting people and property
  • Conducting routine patrols with the team
  • Ensuring the security, safety and well-being of all personnel, visitors, and the premises
  • Immediately responding to emergencies to provide necessary assistance
  • Following procedures for various initiatives, including fire prevention, property patrol, traffic control and accident investigations
  • Writing and submitting security reports
  • Ensuring all incident/accident reports are recorded accurately and submitted to your manager as they occur

YES. Norpro Guards are required to wear assigned uniforms and Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) related to the type of guard. All Industrial Guards are required to wear:
  • Hard-hat (with hearing protection depending on the job)
  • High-vis fire-resistant shirt with the Norpro logo on the left side of the chest
  • ID badge on the right side of chest
  • High-vis fire-resistant jacket
  • High-vis fire-resistant pants
  • Steel toe boots

Our Industrial Security Guards are scheduled to work on 7-day and/or 14-day shift rotations and work 12-hour shifts throughout the rotation

While working on site, all accommodations and meals are covered at no cost to the employee.

We offer free shuttle services to and from job sites, or we provide mileage reimbursement for commutes over 85 kilometers.

Norpro provides Industrial Security Services 3-5 hours north of Sault Ste. Marie.

Our Industrial Guards receive wage increases after 6 months and again at 1 year in the field.

Yes. Norpro offers an Employee Referral Bonus to all staff/employees.

Any employee who refers a candidate that is selected to fill a position as an Industrial Security Guard with Norpro and completes 1 month in the field will receive a gift card valued at $250.00.
