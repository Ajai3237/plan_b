About Us

Our Story.
Welcome to the legacy of Norpro, Established in 1980, Norpro emerged as a prominent security company with a singular mission: to deliver high-quality security solutions to our valued clients. Over the years, Norpro's commitment to excellence has driven us to evolve beyond conventional security services. Our story is one of growth, adaptability, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. From our humble beginnings in the security sector, we have expanded our horizons to become a comprehensive security solutions provider in the region. Join us on this journey, where security meets innovation, and where Norpro continues to redefine the boundaries of service excellence.
Mine Security
Norpro excels in securing the mining sector in Northern Ontario. Our dedicated industrial security guards, licensed in Ontario and trained in security awareness, guarantee a secure environment. From medical first response to defensive driving, CANNAMM drug testing, and live monitoring, our team excels in access control, refinery security, and crisis intervention.
Patrol
Our diligent patrol guards create a secure environment for customers and employees through expert monitoring of premises and personnel. Specially trained for mobile patrol inspections, our officers, whether on foot or by car, conduct thorough spot checks, parking lot patrols, and perimeter sweeps. With advanced tools, they provide real-time online reporting, ensuring enhanced security for your peace of mind.
Institutional Guards
Norpro excels in tailored Institutional Security Services for education, healthcare, and government institutions. Our expert team ensures a secure environment for students, staff, patients, and the public. With skilled professionals, strict access control, swift emergency response, advanced surveillance, efficient incident reporting, and custom solutions, Norpro stands out for its comprehensive and professional approach.

Static Guards & Event Management
At Norpro, we specialize in providing professional static guards and event management security services to your needs. Our trained guards ensure the safety and protection of your premises, whether for long-term assignments or one-off events. With a keen focus on crowd control, risk assessment, and maintaining order, our security team is equipped to handle events of any scale. From corporate gatherings to large public events, Norpro delivers reliable security solutions, giving you peace of mind. Trust us to safeguard your event with our expert security services.
Industrial Guards
We prioritize precision and expertise in safeguarding your operations. Our highly trained personnel ensure the safety of industrial facilities, assets, and personnel through advanced, sector-specific security measures. From access control to incident response, our skilled professionals, trained in industrial security, adeptly handle the complexities of your environment.
Loss Prevention
This is dedicated to minimizing losses and protecting assets for businesses across Northern Ontario. Our team employs a multi-faceted approach, including video surveillance, access control, inventory audits, and employee training programs. By identifying vulnerabilities, implementing proactive measures, and conducting regular assessments, we help mitigate risks and safeguard your business against financial losses.

Other Services
We understand that security is not a one-size-fits-all solution, and we are committed to tailoring our services to your unique needs. Our team of highly trained professionals is dedicated to making your safety and security our top priority. From loss prevention and crowd control to cash in transit and coordinating First Nation Gatherings, we have the expertise and experience to ensure that you, your assets and your interests are safeguarded. With a commitment to dependability, integrity, and excellence, we provide peace of mind, so you can focus on what matters most.
Respirator Fit Testing
Trust us to deliver precision in protection, safeguarding your workforce against airborne hazards with expert fit testing and comprehensive respiratory protection solutions. Partner with Norpro for a workplace where respiratory safety is never compromised.
Training
Norpro’s team of Health & Safety and Industrial Hygiene specialists have years of experience delivering training material within organizations, at public conferences and via online platforms. Norpro has developed a suite of general safety and hygiene training packages that are available to be delivered at your company site, as well as having the ability to develop specific training packages to meet your company’s needs.

Support Services
Health and Safety (H&S) in the workplace is essential for protecting employees and ensuring legal compliance. However, many workplaces lack dedicated resources for effective H&S management. Norpro H&S can support your team in maintaining compliance and fostering a safer work environment.
Management System Implementation & Auditing
A Management System ensures efficiency, compliance, and goal achievement in an organization. Norpro H&S supports all stages, from planning and design to implementation, monitoring, and continuous improvement. Let us help streamline your H&S management system!
Hazardous Materials Testing, Management & Remediation
Hazardous materials in construction can pose risks to workers and the public. Ontario Regulation 490/09 under the Occupational Health and Safety Act defines designated substances as hazardous materials requiring controlled exposure. Ontario recognizes 11 designated substances, including asbestos, lead, mercury, and silica.

Indoor Air Quality (IAQ) Assessment Services & Remediation
Indoor Air Quality (IAQ) refers to the physical and chemical characteristics of indoor air, impacting occupant health and comfort. Poor IAQ can cause issues like headaches, eye irritation, and stuffiness, often linked to building-related illness (BRI) or sick-building syndrome (SBS).
Workplace Noise Assessments
Noise is a common workplace hazard that can cause stress, hearing loss, and safety risks by interfering with communication. Ontario Regulation 351/15 under the Occupational Health & Safety Act requires employers to protect workers from hazardous sound levels.
