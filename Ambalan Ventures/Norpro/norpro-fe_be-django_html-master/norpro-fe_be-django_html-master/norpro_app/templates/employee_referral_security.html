Terms & Conditions

Awards/Incentives

To reward employees who participate in this program, we provide the following incentives:


  • The referring employee will receive $250 cash bonus after the new employee has reached their 90-day milestone.
  • After 6 months, the referring employee will receive an additional $250 if the new hire remains with the company.
  • After 1 year, the referring employee will receive an additional $250.

The employee who makes the referral must still be employed with Norpro Security Ltd to receive the bonus.


How to Make a Referral

Refer employees by filling the form above or reach out to HR at hr@norpro.ca to obtain the Employee Referral Form”. The individual you are referring will need to send their application to hiring@norpro.ca.


In case the same resume/candidate referral is received from more than one employee, date and time of the receipt of the resume will be considered and the person who has sent the resume first will be eligible for the bonus Employees referring the candidate should obtain the candidate’s consent before forwarding a candidates resume to Human Resources.


Eligibility

With the exception of hiring managers, recruitment staff and the senior management, all employees, regardless of tenure are eligible for the referral bonus/awards.The referred person cannot be a former employee.


Eligible Positions and Vacancies

Only permanent full-time position vacancies would meet the criteria for the referral.


Payment of Award

Employees will receive their award on dates as outlined above for the pay- period in which the anniversary falls. The award will be added to their regular pay and will be subject to applicable taxes.


Termination of Program

Norpro Security Ltd reserves the right to end or modify the Employee Referral Program at any time to meet the needs of the business. If the Program is terminated, Norpro would honor the referrals already in process.
