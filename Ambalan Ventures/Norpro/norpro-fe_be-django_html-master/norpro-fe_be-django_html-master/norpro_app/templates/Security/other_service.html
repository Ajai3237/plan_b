Overview

We understand that security is not a one-size-fits-all solution, and we are committed to tailoring our services to your unique needs. Our team of highly trained professionals is dedicated to making your safety and security our top priority. From loss prevention and crowd control to cash in transit and coordinating First Nation Gatherings, we have the expertise and experience to ensure that you, your assets and your interests are safeguarded. With a commitment to dependability, integrity, and excellence, we provide peace of mind, so you can focus on what matters most.

Our Services

Cash in Transit

Norpro offers a robust Cash-in-Transit Security Service, ensuring the safe and secure transport of valuables. Our highly trained professionals implement strategic measures and cutting-edge technology to safeguard cash and assets during transit. With Norpro's commitment to proactive security measures, trust us for reliable and comprehensive Cash-in-Transit Security that sets the standard for excellence in protecting your valuable assets.

Samsara

Our Samsara Service offers advanced GPS fleet tracking and real-time vehicle monitoring to enhance the safety and efficiency of your operations. Using the latest technology, we provide real-time location updates, driver behavior monitoring, and vehicle diagnostics, ensuring the security of your assets on the move. Whether managing a fleet of service vehicles or heavy equipment, Samsara allows you to track routes, prevent unauthorized use, and respond quickly to any incidents. With detailed insights and alerts, you can ensure optimal fleet performance and security at all times.

Guarda: Live Monitoring

Through our collaboration with Gardaworld, Norpro offers the Guarda – Live Monitoring service, ensuring continuous, real-time surveillance for enhanced security. This partnership allows us to provide 24/7 monitoring of your premises, ensuring swift responses to potential threats or incidents. With new technology and Gardaworld’s expertise in live monitoring, we guarantee heightened protection for businesses of all sizes. Whether you need to monitor remote sites, sensitive areas, or high-risk locations, we offers unmatched reliability and proactive security solutions, keeping your assets and personnel safe around the clock.
